About the Project

Predictable Paul is a daily ritual of meme-coin launches and culture drops, all revolving around one character: Predictable Paul. Every night at 9:00 PM EST, a new Paul is born on Solana — and this cycle repeats for 365 straight days. Across the year: 365 Paul coins will launch, each one as predictable as the clock. Leaderboards track every Paul by peak market cap. After one year, the Top 99 Predictable Pauls will be canonized into the official NFT collection. Trader performance is tracked across all Paul coins, with rewards distributed from the Treasury to top performers. It’s simple: same time, new Paul, every day — building out our brand in predictable, gamified fashion.

  • Daily Paul drop at 9:00 PM EST
  • Public Top-10 leaderboard by peak market cap
  • Top 99 Pauls become a curated NFT collection (1 year after Paul #1)
  • Trader tracking & rewards (coming soon)
  • Creator fee treasury: transparent & on-chain

Predictable Paul — The NFT Collection

One year after $PP1, we snapshot the historical leaderboard and mint a curated collection of the Top 99 Predictable Pauls. Each NFT is a canonized chapter in the Paul mythos with on-chain metadata: rank, peak MC, date, and provenance. The holder of each NFT will become the owner and leader of the underlying Predictable Paul memecoin.
