About the Project

Predictable Paul is a daily cadence of meme-coin launches and culture drops, all orbiting a single character: Paul. Each day at 9:00 PM EST, a new Paul goes live. We track market-cap peaks, crown the legends, and build a brand universe around the most iconic runs. Everything feeds a transparent treasury and future rewards.
